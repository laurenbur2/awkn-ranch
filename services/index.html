Three Ways to Care for Yourself Here

Always-on amenities, by-appointment add-ons, and a sister practice for psychedelic therapy.

Members and overnight guests have full use of the wellness facilities. Add-ons are booked through us and held by a practitioner. Ceremonial ketamine therapy is offered through our sister practice, Within Center, located on the ranch.

Our Sister Practice

Within Center.

Ceremonial ketamine therapy, held on the ranch.

Within Center is the medical practice that lives alongside AWKN. A licensed clinical team holds ceremonial ketamine therapy, medication management, and integration support — in the same temple, with the same care, on the same land.

For guests who want to layer the medicine work into a stay, we coordinate the two together. Most return to AWKN afterward to rest in it.

Amenities

Open to every guest.

The wellness facilities and lodging that come with any stay or membership. No appointment, no add-on charge — use them as much as you want.

Heat Therapy
Cedar Sauna
Traditional barrel sauna under the live oaks. Open daily, fits up to six. Pair with the cold plunge for a proper contrast cycle.
Contrast
Cold Plunge & Hot Tub
A 38-45°F plunge tub paired with a deep hot tub, framed by the wellness courtyard. The most-used space on the property.
Open Water
Pool
A long swimming pool for laps in the morning, lounging in the afternoon. Heated through the cooler months.
Practice Space
Wellness Center
The dedicated building for bodywork, integration, and one-on-one sessions. Quiet rooms, soft light, and a tea bar for between visits.
Movement
Pickleball Court
A full-size pickleball court tucked between the gardens. Paddles and balls in the basket courtside — play whenever you like.
Rest
Hammock Garden
A grove of strung hammocks under the oaks — the quietest, most-claimed afternoon napping spot on the ranch.
Lodging
Retreat House
The main residence. Private and shared rooms, a chef's kitchen, a wraparound porch, and the long farmhouse table where everyone eats.
Lodging
Yurts
Private circular cabins tucked into the trees. Wood floors, soft beds, windows full of sky. For guests who want quiet.
Lodging & Studio
Geodesic Dome
A geodesic shelter at the edge of the meadow. Doubles as overnight lodging and a movement studio for retreat groups.
Add-Ons

By appointment.

Sessions held by a practitioner, layered into your stay. Book in advance — calendars fill quickly during retreat weeks.

Bodywork
60-Minute Massage
Deep tissue, Swedish, or somatic-experiencing-informed touch. Held in a private room of the Retreat House.
Coaching
Integration Coaching
A 60-minute session with one of our integration coaches. Useful before or after a ceremony, or at the end of a retreat.
Reading
Human Design Reading
A personalized reading of your Human Design chart. Useful for understanding your patterns of energy and decision-making.
Therapy
Licensed Therapy Session
A one-hour session with a licensed therapist on staff. Talk therapy, IFS, or trauma-focused work depending on need.
Reading
Astrology Session
A natal-chart reading and conversation about transits. For curiosity, decision-points, or the texture of a season.
Ceremony
Hapé Ceremony
A held container for hapé (sacred Amazonian snuff), used for grounding, clearing, and intention-setting.
Therapy
Internal Family Systems Session
An IFS-modality session for working with parts. Held by a trained therapist; particularly useful in retreat container.
Sound
Private Sound Journey
A one-on-one or couples sound bath. Crystal bowls, gongs, voice — held in the temple, just for you.
Movement
Pickleball Lesson
Yes, really. We have a court. Schedule a lesson with our resident teacher, or just play.

Ready to add something on?
