Our Services

Offerings for the body and spirit.

Layer your stay with the practices that meet you where you are. Each session is held by a practitioner, not booked through an app.
A Living Menu

Choose what your stay needs.

All offerings are available a-la-carte to overnight guests, day visitors, and members. Practitioners rotate seasonally; this list reflects what's currently available.

Movement Sound Contrast Bodywork Ceremony Medical
Movement

Yoga, breath, somatics

Yoga & Mindful Movement

60 min Group or 1:1 From $35

Slow vinyasa, yin, and restorative classes held in the temple or under the oaks. Trained for trauma-informed practice; suitable for first-timers and longtime practitioners alike.

Daily group classes 7am & 5pm. Private sessions by appointment.

Breathwork & Meditation

45–75 min Group or 1:1 From $45

Pranayama, holotropic-style sessions, and silent sits. Held in the temple with cushions, blankets, and intentional pacing — designed to be safe to fall apart in.

Group breathwork ceremonies on Saturday evenings; private sessions on request.

Sound

Listening as medicine

Sound Bath

60 min Group up to 16 From $55

Quartz crystal bowls, gongs, voice. Lie down, cover up, and let the room become a single instrument. One of the most-requested sessions on the ranch.

Held weekly in the temple; private sessions for retreat groups.

Contrast Therapy

Heat, cold, and rest

Cedar Sauna

Open daily Up to 6 Included

A traditional barrel sauna under the live oaks. Authentic dry heat with the option to layer in essential oils or guided rounds.

Open to all overnight guests & members; day-pass holders welcome.

Cold Plunge

Open daily Solo or group Included

A contrast cold tub kept between 38–45°F year-round, paired with the sauna for proper hot-cold cycling. Guided plunge instruction available for first-timers.

Bodywork

Touch, presence, release

Massage & Somatic Bodywork

60 / 90 / 120 min 1:1 From $145

Deep tissue, Swedish, and somatic-experiencing-informed bodywork with our resident practitioners. Held in a private room of the Retreat House.

Book in advance — sessions fill quickly during retreat weeks.

Ceremony

Ritual at the edge of the woods

Fire Circle

2 hours Group up to 30 From $35

A held container around the fire pit. Songs, silence, and the structure of intention — for retreat closings, full moons, or simply because it's Saturday.

Temple Ceremony

2–4 hours Group or private By inquiry

Cacao, plant-medicine-aligned (no medicine), or simply a held silence in the temple. We work with visiting facilitators and our own resident guides.

Medical & Therapeutic

Sister Practice: Within Center

For ceremonial ketamine therapy, medication management, and clinical psychiatric care, we partner with our sister practice Within Center, located at AWKN Ranch.

Within is a fully licensed medical practice; the AWKN Ranch wellness offerings on this page are non-medical and complementary.
