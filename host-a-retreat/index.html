Host a Retreat

Bring your container here.

Teachers, facilitators, and small groups: rent the ranch in full or in part. We hold the lodging, meals, and spaces — you bring the work.
For Facilitators

A retreat venue, not a hotel.

AWKN was built by people who run retreats. Every detail — the temple acoustics, the kitchen flow, the timing of breakfast, where the cushions live — was designed for the work. Bring your group; we handle the rest.

What's Included

Everything you'd have to source

Lodging for up to 22

Private and shared rooms, two yurts, and two geodesic domes. Most guests have their own bed; almost everyone has a window.

The Temple

A dedicated ceremonial space with cushions, candles, sound system, and natural light. Capacity 40 seated, 16 lying.

Chef-Built Kitchen

Bring your own chef, or hire one of ours. Commercial-grade range, walk-in pantry, and a long farmhouse table.

Wellness Facilities

Cedar sauna, cold plunge, fire pit, gardens, and the Honey Dome studio — all included with the buyout, all open during your retreat.

On-Site Concierge

A live-in caretaker handles the logistics during your retreat — firewood, linens, garbage, late-night requests — so you can stay in role.

Optional Add-Ons

We can layer in bodywork, sound healing, ceremonial offerings, or our medical team from Within Center — if your container calls for it.

Capacity

How big can your group be?

Most retreats land between 8 and 16 guests. The temple comfortably holds 40 in seated practice, and the property maxes out at 22 overnight.
