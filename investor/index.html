10 beds — private rooms at $499/night, shared at $349/night. Open to wellness seekers, retreat groups, and event attendees. 10% discount for one-week outpatient clients.

$15K–$22K/mo at 70–80% occ.

Stream 2: Yurts, Domes & Maloka

$8K–$16K/mo

$16.5K–$33K/mo

$20K–$75K/mo

Stream 6: Memberships
