Capital · Development Budget

Where the $29.9M Goes

A fully-loaded development budget — hard costs, soft costs, and a 10% contingency. The program uses ~75% of the site's impervious-coverage cap, leaving a comfortable buffer.

Impervious coverage check. 8.5 acres (370,260 sf) · 25% cap = 92,565 sf allowed. Domes carry a 50% deck credit (built on decks with pervious natural trails). Total impervious used: ~69,500 sf — 75.1% of cap, a comfortable buffer.
Honest caveats. Construction costs are current estimates, not yet GC-bid. The program assumes the City confirms the impervious-coverage strategy (in process). A strong concept stage, firming toward fundable.
The Return · 15-Year Hold

Unlevered, Before Financing

The real estate is modeled as a 15-year hold with no leverage assumed — a deliberately conservative base case. Cash flow compounds while the asset appreciates; debt financing or a cash-out refinance would amplify equity returns from here.

Key Assumptions & Methodology

Engine One — Within | AWKN Operating Business

  • Retreats: blended price $4,950 · ~4 clients/week · medical/Katie cost $880/client · 6% sales commission
  • Weekend events: ~$10,000/weekend Year 1 · 10% commission · Maloka dome & new lodging domes add venue + nightly income
  • Base monthly overhead: $59,070 (core team, facilities, food, land lease/mortgage, insurance, marketing, leadership)
  • Post-raise additions: dedicated sales role ($4,000/mo) + paid marketing ($10,000/mo), deployed ~Month 3
  • Annual revenue growth 17.5% · annual cost inflation 5%
  • Year 1 reflects steady run-rate; first quarter ramps as the raise is deployed

Engine Two — Live Well / Dome Collective Real Estate

  • 70 domes at $3,000/mo avg · 18 luxury apartments at $3,500/mo avg · 95% stabilized occupancy
  • Restaurant leased out: $5,000/mo base + ~$3,000/mo profit share = ~$9,000/mo income (they operate it)
  • Operating expense ratio 35% of residential revenue · annual rent growth 3% · expense inflation 3%
  • Build cost: domes $100K each · Longevity Center + 18 apts 42,000 sf @ $300/sf
  • Parking: 75 underground spaces @ $38K + 10 surface spaces @ $5K (incl. 2 ADA)
  • Soft costs 12% · contingency 10% · total project cost ~$29.9M
  • Stabilized value capitalized at ~5.5% market cap rate
