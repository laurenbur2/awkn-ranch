The Journey

How the Week Unfolds

Consultation

A free phone call with our clinical team to discuss your goals, medical history, and whether the immersion is the right container for you.

Medical Clearance

Intake and screening with our MAPS-trained nurse practitioner to ensure safe, personalized care for the week ahead.

Arrival & Preparation

Sunday check-in at AWKN Ranch. Settle into your room, meet your concierge, and begin a dedicated preparation arc to set intention and establish safety.

Ceremony & Integration

Two guided ketamine ceremonies in the honeycomb dome, each followed by integration coaching to translate insight into lasting change.

Rest & Restoration

Sauna, cold plunge, hot tub, resort pool, breakfast on the porch, and the quiet of twelve private acres. The ranch is yours to roam.

Closing & Departure

Saturday morning, we close the container together. You leave with a personalized integration plan and an open door to continued outpatient care.
