Psychedelic Therapy in Austin, Texas

Legal, ceremonial ketamine therapy — the psychedelic-assisted path to healing anxiety, depression, trauma, and burnout. Held with intention, supported by integration, grounded in licensed medical care.

If you have been searching for psychedelic therapy in Austin, you have likely found a confusing landscape. Headlines speak of psilocybin and MDMA breakthroughs, but most of those medicines remain federally restricted. The question becomes: what psychedelic-assisted therapy is actually available in Texas right now, and what is it like?

At Within Center, we offer ceremonial ketamine therapy — the only legal, psychedelic-assisted medicine available in Austin. Ketamine is FDA-approved, used safely for decades, and produces a non-ordinary state of consciousness similar in many ways to classical psychedelics. Held in the right container, with the right preparation and the right integration, it can do profound therapeutic work.

What is psychedelic therapy?

Psychedelic-assisted therapy uses a non-ordinary state of consciousness — produced by a medicine like ketamine, psilocybin, MDMA, or DMT — as a doorway into deeper psychological work. The medicine quiets the mind's usual defenses. Old patterns can loosen. Long-held grief, anxiety, and stuck stories can be met with tenderness instead of resistance.

What separates psychedelic therapy from recreational use is the framework: a careful preparation phase, an intentional session held by trained clinicians, and an integration phase afterward where insights become lasting change.

Which psychedelic therapies are legal in Austin?

Currently in Texas, only ketamine is legally available for therapeutic use outside of formal clinical trials. Psilocybin (psychedelic mushrooms), MDMA, DMT, and ayahuasca remain federally restricted. While some of these medicines are in late-stage clinical research, they are not yet a path you can walk in Austin.

Ketamine, by contrast, has been used safely in medicine since the 1970s. It received FDA recognition for treatment-resistant depression in 2019. When held ceremonially — with preparation, intention, and integration — it functions as a true psychedelic-assisted therapy.

Frequently asked questions about psychedelic therapy in Austin

Is psychedelic therapy legal in Austin, Texas?

Ketamine-assisted psychedelic therapy is legal in Austin and throughout Texas. Psilocybin, MDMA, and DMT are not currently legal for therapeutic use in Texas outside of formal clinical trials.

What conditions does psychedelic therapy treat?

Treatment-resistant depression, anxiety, PTSD, complex trauma, burnout, grief, and existential or spiritual distress. Every client begins with a clinical consultation to determine whether the work is the right fit.

How is ceremonial ketamine therapy different from a ketamine clinic?

Most ketamine clinics offer brief IV infusions in a clinical setting. Ceremonial ketamine therapy at Within Center is held as a psychedelic-assisted experience: longer sessions, intentional preparation, a held container, music, and integration coaching afterward — all under licensed medical supervision.

Do you offer psilocybin, MDMA, or DMT therapy in Austin?

No. Psilocybin, MDMA, and DMT remain federally restricted and are not legally available for therapeutic use in Texas. We work exclusively with ketamine — the only legal psychedelic medicine currently available — held in a ceremonial, integration-supported framework.

How much does psychedelic therapy in Austin cost?

Within Center offers several packages, from a single-ceremony Discover experience to multi-ceremony arcs and full residential retreats at AWKN Ranch. Specific pricing is shared during your free consultation, where we help match you to the right level of care.
