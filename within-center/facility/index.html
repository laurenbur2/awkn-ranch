AWKN Ranch · West Austin

Our Facility

A 12-acre private wellness sanctuary in West Austin, 15 minutes from downtown — where every detail is designed for deep healing work.

The Setting

Held at AWKN Ranch

Within Center lives at AWKN Ranch — a private sanctuary on the western edge of Austin. Live oaks, stone terraces, dedicated ceremony spaces, and world-class wellness amenities, all on a quiet 12 acres designed to hold you as you do the work.

Ceremony Space

The Temple

Our primary ceremony space — a dedicated building designed to hold sacred work. Floor-level seating, curated sound, warm wood, and intentional decor create a container that feels both grounded and expansive.

This is where guided ketamine ceremonies are held for both day experience clients and retreat guests.

Ceremony Space

The Honeycomb Dome

A geodesic dome nestled under the oaks — flooded with natural light and designed for intimate, private ceremony work. Cushions, blankets, and a quiet stillness that invites you inward.

Wellness Amenities

Rest, Restore, Integrate

The ranch is designed to support your nervous system before, during, and after ceremony.

Barrel Sauna

Wood-fired barrel sauna for deep heat, relaxation, and nervous system regulation

Cold Plunge

Hot Tub

Warm water soaking for integration and unwinding after deep work

Resort Pool

Sound Healing Beds

12 Acres of Grounds

Mature live oaks, stone terraces, walking paths, and hammock gardens

Retreat Accommodations

Private Yurts & Guest Rooms

Retreat guests live on the ranch in private yurts nestled under the oaks or in dedicated guest rooms in the main lodge. Each space is simple, quiet, and designed to let the nervous system rest.
