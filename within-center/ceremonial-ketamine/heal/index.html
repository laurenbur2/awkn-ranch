Common Questions

What to Know

Why three ceremonies?

The nervous system needs repetition and safety to truly shift. One ceremony can open the door; three give you time to walk through it. Most meaningful change happens between sessions, in integration.

How far apart are the ceremonies?

Typically 1–3 weeks apart, depending on your integration pace. We adjust the rhythm to what your body and life can hold.

What does the AWKN membership include?

Access to the sauna, cold plunge, red light, sound beds, community events, and the grounds at AWKN Ranch — supportive practices that reinforce the nervous system work you are doing here.
