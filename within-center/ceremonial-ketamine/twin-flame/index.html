Who This Is For

Is Couples Reset Right for You?

Couples Reset is for partners who love each other and want to do the work together — whether you are navigating a hard season, recovering from a rupture, or simply ready to know each other more deeply. You do not need to be in crisis. You just need to be ready to be honest, and to be seen.

Common Questions

What to Know

Do we journey at the same time?

Yes. You both receive the medicine in the same container, with guides holding the space for both of you. Each of your experiences is your own, but you are never separated.

What if only one of us wants to do this?

Both partners need to fully choose this work. If one of you is uncertain, we recommend beginning with a consultation so we can explore together whether this is the right time.

Is this therapy?

This is not couples therapy in the traditional sense — we are not facilitating conversations about your relationship. We are holding a ceremonial container in which each of you can meet yourselves honestly, which often transforms the relationship from the inside out.
