Who This Is For

Is Twin Flame Right for You?

Twin Flame is for partners who love each other and want to do the work together — whether you are navigating a hard season, recovering from a rupture, or simply ready to know each other more deeply. You do not need to be in crisis. You just need to be ready to be honest, and to be seen.

The Arc

How the Twin Flame Journey Unfolds

Free Consultation

A conversation with both of you to understand where you are, what you are hoping for, and whether this container can hold what you are carrying.

Individual Medical Clearance

Each partner completes their own intake and screening with our MAPS-trained nurse practitioner to ensure the work is safe for both of you.

Shared Preparation

A session where you and your partner clarify what you are working on together, set a shared intention, and prepare for the ceremony.

The Ceremony

A private guided ketamine ceremony held with both of you in the same sacred container. You journey side by side, supported throughout, and emerge into shared stillness.

Integration

Two integration sessions — time to reflect individually and together, to speak honestly about what arose, and to carry the work into your daily life as partners.

Common Questions

What to Know

Do we journey at the same time?

Yes. You both receive the medicine in the same container, with guides holding the space for both of you. Each of your experiences is your own, but you are never separated.

What if only one of us wants to do this?

Both partners need to fully choose this work. If one of you is uncertain, we recommend beginning with a consultation so we can explore together whether this is the right time.

Is this therapy?

This is not couples therapy in the traditional sense — we are not facilitating conversations about your relationship. We are holding a ceremonial container in which each of you can meet yourselves honestly, which often transforms the relationship from the inside out.
