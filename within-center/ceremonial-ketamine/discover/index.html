Who This Is For

Is Discover Right for You?

Discover is designed for the curious — for people who have been quietly wondering whether this work is for them but want to begin without committing to a full series. It is also for those who want to experience the container before choosing a deeper path.

Common Questions

What to Know

Is one session enough?

For some, yes — Discover is designed to be a complete, stand-alone arc. For others, it is the beginning of deeper work. We will help you understand what you need, and there is no pressure to commit beyond what feels right.

What does the ceremony feel like?

Every journey is different. Most clients describe a softening — a quieting of the mental chatter, a sense of spaciousness, and access to feelings and insights that are usually held just out of reach. You remain safe and held throughout.

Can I drive afterward?

No. You will need someone to drive you home, or you can arrange a ride. We can also help coordinate a stay at AWKN Ranch next door if you would prefer a softer landing.
