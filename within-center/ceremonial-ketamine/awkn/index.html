Common Questions

What to Know

How long does the full arc take?

Most clients complete AWKN over 3–8 weeks, pacing their ceremonies based on readiness and integration capacity. We shape the rhythm to your life.

Is this the right package for me?

AWKN is for people who are ready for deep transformation — who want to create new habits, break old patterns, and change the trajectory of their life. If you are seeking real breakthrough and lasting change, this is the container built for that.

What if I need support between sessions?

AWKN clients have direct access to their guide between ceremonies for questions, check-ins, and support as integration unfolds. You are not alone in the work.
