Common Questions

What to Know

How long does the full arc take?

Most clients complete AWKN over 3–5 months, pacing their ceremonies 2–4 weeks apart. We shape the rhythm to your life and integration capacity.

Is this the right package for me?

AWKN is deep work. It is not the place to start if you have never done this kind of journey before — we would recommend Discover or Heal first. But if you have done the work and are ready to go further, this is the container built for that.

What if I need support between sessions?

AWKN clients have direct access to their guide between ceremonies for questions, check-ins, and support as integration unfolds. You are not alone in the work.
