About Us
Sacred Medicine, Met with Rigor

The Within Center was built by people who believe medicine and meaning belong in the same room. We hold ketamine as the sacred tool it is — and we hold the people in our care with the discernment, training, and intention that the work deserves.

We are a sister practice to AWKN Ranch, sharing values of intentional living, embodied healing, and quiet reverence for the inner journey. Clients may choose to extend their experience by staying at the ranch — not as treatment, but as a supportive environment for integration, regulation, and reflection.
