Medication Management

Thoughtful psychiatric care, when you need it.

Board-certified psychiatry — for depression, anxiety, PTSD, ADHD, and complex mental health conditions. Available as a stand-alone service or alongside ceremonial ketamine work.

Medication can be a meaningful part of recovery — but only when it is prescribed by someone who is actually paying attention. At Within Center, our psychiatric care is unhurried, personalized, and focused on helping you feel steady enough to do the deeper work of healing.

We see medication as one tool among many. Our role is to help you understand your options, weigh the trade-offs, and find a regimen that supports the life you actually want to live.

What We Treat

Conditions We Support

Our psychiatrists work with adults seeking thoughtful, ongoing care for the conditions that most often go under-treated — including depression that hasn't fully responded to first-line medications, anxiety, and chronic burnout.

  • Treatment-resistant depression — depression that hasn't fully lifted with standard antidepressants, where it's worth taking a more careful, individualized approach.
  • Anxiety disorders — generalized anxiety, panic, social anxiety, and OCD.
  • Burnout, irritability, and sleep disruption — the chronic states that often hide under bigger labels and quietly erode daily life.
  • PTSD and trauma-related symptoms — stress responses tied to past experiences, supported through steady outpatient care.
  • ADHD — in adults, with diagnostic clarification when needed.
  • Bipolar spectrum and mood regulation — with careful, conservative prescribing.

Every new patient begins with a thorough medical and psychiatric evaluation, so we can make sure our approach is the right fit for what you're navigating.

How It Works

A Simple Path to Care

01

Initial Consultation

A 60-minute intake with one of our psychiatrists to understand your history, current symptoms, and goals.

02

Personalized Plan

If medication is appropriate, we build a plan together — with full transparency about benefits, side effects, and alternatives.

03

Ongoing Support

Regular follow-up visits to monitor response, adjust as needed, and stay connected over time.
