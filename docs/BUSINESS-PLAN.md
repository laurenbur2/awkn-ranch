## Executive Summary

AWKN Ranch is a 12-acre wellness sanctuary in Austin, Texas, seeking $500,000 via SAFE note at an $8M valuation cap. The organization projects reaching profitability within 6-9 months, with conservative annual net profit of $679,716, base case of $1.536M, and strong year projections exceeding $2.1M.

The business represents a strategic pivot from Within Center's original 30-day inpatient model to a diversified ecosystem combining nightly lodging, outpatient therapy, venue rentals, and membership programs across four revenue pillars: Wellness, Retreats & Events, Residential, and Memberships.

---

## The Pivot: Model Evolution

The founders shifted from an inpatient-focused structure because the original model created operational constraints despite strong brand reputation. Within Center operated for 4.5 years, delivered over 1,500 sessions, and maintains five-star ratings across all platforms—establishing clinical credibility that now anchors the broader AWKN brand.

The new model eliminates multi-brand confusion by unifying operations under a single brand identity. Rather than abandoning Within Center's legacy, the rebranding amplifies it: **"One name. One website. One booking flow."**
