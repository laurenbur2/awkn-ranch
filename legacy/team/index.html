Who We Are

A small team, by intention.

AWKN Ranch is run by a tight group of founders, resident practitioners, and visiting facilitators. Most of us live on the land or close to it. Most of us have come to this work through our own healing.

How We Work

A few commitments

01

Slow on Purpose

We will not rush you. The schedule has space; the practitioners have presence; the land has time.

02

No Performance

You don't need to arrive ready, photogenic, or healed. The point of coming here is the part you can't post.

03

Real Practitioners

Every offering is held by a trained, human practitioner — never auto-played. The medicine is in the relationship.
